To join rise2gether, you can participate in several ways depending on your interests and professional background. Based on the sources, here is an organised guide on how you can get involved:

1. Choose Your Role
You can contribute your voice and talents through three primary pathways:

• [Join as a Volunteer](https://www.rise2gether.co/job-board): This role is for those who want to share their time and skills to foster a stronger community and empower Asian women.

• [Join as a Speaker](https://www.rise2gether.co/join-as-speaker): If you have expertise, insights, or a vision for the future, you can join to inspire others by sharing your experiences.

• [Join as a Business Partner](https://www.rise2gether.co/join-as-partner): This is specifically designed for Asian women entrepreneurs. The organisation offers resources and support to help grow your local business.

2. Engage with Programmes and Events
You can also join the community by participating in their specific initiatives, such as:
• [The Leadership Programme](https://www.rise2gether.co/r2-leadership-program).
• [The Asian Women Summit](https://www.rise2gether.co/rising-together-summit-2025).
• [Career Panel Events](https://www.rise2gether.co/event).
• [General Membership opportunities](https://www.rise2gether.co/membership)

3. Stay Connected and Informed
To begin the process or stay updated on new opportunities, the sources suggest the following actions:
• Visit the "Join Us" section on their website to access specific sign-up options for volunteers, speakers, or partners.
• Subscribe to their updates to receive the latest information.
• Follow their social media channels to engage with the community online, including LinkedIn, YouTube, Instagram, and RedNote (RED).
In essence, joining this community is like adding your unique thread to a tapestry; whether you are providing the strength of the fabric as a volunteer, the bright colours of wisdom as a speaker, or expanding the pattern as a business partner, your contribution helps complete a larger picture of empowerment
