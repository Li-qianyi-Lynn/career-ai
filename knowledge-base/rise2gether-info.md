# Rise2gether Information

## What is Rise2gether?

Rise2gether is a non-profit organization (NPO) dedicated to helping people grow and advance in their career development. We invite successful guests from various industries to share their career experiences, challenges, and success stories, providing valuable career guidance and support to community members. The organization has 300 volunteers and about 4,000 followers online. Volunteers are key to the organization's existence. Senior woman leaders have expressed a willingness to share with the community. The organization will be starting to do more online events to connect the leaders.

## Mission

RISE2GETHER is a non-profit organization based in Seattle, WA. We are committed to advancing the careers of Asian women. We build community, open doors, and drive change - empowering Asian women to dream, create and lead.

## Core Activities

1. **Guest Speaker Events**: Regularly invite guests from different industries and backgrounds to share their career experiences
2. **Career Guidance**: Provide advice on career planning, interview skills, industry insights, and more
3. **Community Support**: Build a supportive and learning community network

## Target Audience

- Young people seeking career direction
- Professionals looking to transition careers
- Working professionals seeking career growth and advancement
- Students and graduates interested in career development

## Values

- **Authenticity**: Share real and meaningful career stories
- **Inclusivity**: Welcome people from diverse backgrounds
- **Growth**: Continuous learning and improvement
- **Community**: Mutual support and assistance
